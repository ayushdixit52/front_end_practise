meet tom

A Tom cat, or male cat, is known for its playful and sometimes independent nature. These furry companions, with their sharp claws and keen senses, are skilled hunters of small prey. A cat's sleek body, attentive ears, and gentle purr make it a beloved pet. Though they sleep a lot during the day, Tom cats become active at night, often entertaining themselves by playing with small objects or chasing after toys. They are intelligent, communicate with humans through soft meows, and provide comfort and joy to their families.
